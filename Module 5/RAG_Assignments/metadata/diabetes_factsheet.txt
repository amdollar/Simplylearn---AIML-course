Diabetes Factsheet

Diabetes is a chronic disease characterized by elevated blood glucose levels. The main types are type 1, type 2, and gestational diabetes.

**Management**: Diabetes management includes a balanced diet, regular exercise, blood glucose monitoring, and medication (such as insulin or oral hypoglycemic agents). Complications can be prevented with good control.

**Symptoms**: Increased thirst, frequent urination, unexplained weight loss, and fatigue.

**Prevention**: Maintain a healthy weight, exercise regularly, and have regular health checkups.

For personalized guidance, consult a healthcare professional.
